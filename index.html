INGREDIENTS:

  • Whole wheat flour
  • Grated mozzarella cheese(1 1/4 cups)
  • Chopped bell peppers(1/2 cup)
  • Chopped onions(1/2 cup)
  • Corn kernels(1/4 cup)
  • Chopped olives(8-10)
  • Italian seasoning
  • Chilli flakes(Optional)
  • Pizza sauce
  • Pesto sauce
  • Cooking oil
  • Salt
  • Water

RECIPE:

  1. Take the flour and add salt and water.And then make dough.
  2. Keep the dough aside.
  3. Take the cheese and add the bell peppers, onions, corn, olives and chilli flakes.
  4. Take the dough and roll it into 2 round.
  5. Put the pizza sauce, pesto sauce and italian seasoning on the dough. Now keep the stuffing on it.
  6. Close the dough with the other piece of dough and pressed the corners.
  7. Now shallow fry the stuffed dough, till light brown.
  8. Cut the stuffed one to make it cheesy.
  9. At last yummy pizza paratha is ready to eat.
  10. Serve your guests with yummy sauce.
